Robotics

Robotics Future Technology branch of science and engineering.[1] Robotics involves the design, construction, operation, and use of robots.The goal of robotics is to design machines that can help and assist humans.Robotics integrates fields of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering,information engineering, mechatronics engineering, electronics, biomedical engineering,computer engineering, control systems engineering, software engineering, mathematics, etc.

Service robot

These are robots designed to assist humans in various settings, such as hospitals, airports, and homes. Examples of service robots include the Roomba vacuum cleaner and the PARO therapeutic robot.

Agricultural robot

These are robots that are used in farming and agriculture to perform tasks such as planting, harvesting, and monitoring crops. Examples of agricultural robots include the FarmBot and the Blue River Technology lettuce-thinning robot.

Military robot

These are robots designed for military use, such as reconnaissance, bomb disposal, and battlefield support. Examples of military robots include the iRobot PackBot and the QinetiQ Dragon Runner.
